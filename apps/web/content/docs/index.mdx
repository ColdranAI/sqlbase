# Welcome to SQLBase

SQLBase is a secure, web-based platform that lets you visualize, query, and collaborate on your PostgreSQL databases. No installation required - just sign up and connect your database to get started.

<Callout type="info">
**Ready to start?** Sign up at [sqlbase.com](/) and connect your first database in minutes.
</Callout>

## What is SQLBase?

SQLBase is a hosted database management platform that helps teams:

- **Connect PostgreSQL databases** from anywhere - cloud providers, on-premise, or local
- **Query with confidence** using our intuitive SQL playground with syntax highlighting  
- **Collaborate seamlessly** by organizing work into teams and sharing projects
- **Stay secure** with enterprise-grade encryption and role-based access control
- **Invite anyone** to join your team, even if they don't have an account yet

## How It Works

Getting started with SQLBase is simple:

### 1. Sign Up & Create Organization
- Create your free account at [sqlbase.com](/)
- Set up your first organization for your team
- Choose a name that represents your company or project

### 2. Connect Your Database  
- Add your PostgreSQL connection details
- We support direct connections, SSH tunnels, and VPN access
- Your credentials are encrypted and stored securely

### 3. Start Exploring
- Use the SQL playground to query your data
- Browse your database schema and table statistics
- Export results or save queries for later

### 4. Invite Your Team
- Send email invitations to colleagues
- Assign Admin or Member roles based on their needs
- They'll be set up automatically when they accept

## Why Choose SQLBase?

**🚀 No Setup Required**
Skip the hassle of installing and maintaining local database tools. SQLBase runs in your browser and works from anywhere.

**🔒 Security First**
- All database credentials encrypted with military-grade AES-256-GCM
- Role-based access ensures team members only see what they should
- Support for SSH tunnels and VPN connections for maximum security

**👥 Built for Teams**
- Organization-based collaboration keeps projects organized
- Email invitations make onboarding new team members effortless
- Multiple permission levels ensure proper access control

**⚡ Powerful Yet Simple**
- Intuitive SQL editor with syntax highlighting and auto-completion
- One-click database schema exploration
- Export query results in multiple formats

## Supported Databases

SQLBase currently supports **PostgreSQL** databases from:

- **Cloud Providers**: AWS RDS, Google Cloud SQL, Azure Database
- **Managed Services**: Neon, Supabase, PlanetScale, Railway, Heroku
- **On-Premise**: Your own PostgreSQL servers via SSH or VPN
- **Local Development**: Docker containers and local installations

## Security & Privacy

Your data security is our top priority:

- **Encryption**: All credentials encrypted with AES-256-GCM before storage
- **Zero-Knowledge**: We never see your actual database credentials or data
- **Secure Connections**: All database traffic uses SSL/TLS encryption
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions keep your data secure
- **Audit Logs**: Track who accessed what and when
